How the AI Works
🧠

Minimax Algorithm

The AI explores all possible future game states, assigning scores to terminal states (Win: +10, Lose: -10, Draw: 0) and propagating the optimal values up the game tree.

✂️

Alpha-Beta Pruning

An optimization that eliminates branches that cannot possibly influence the final decision — making the AI faster without sacrificing any accuracy or optimality.

♟️

Game Theory

In Unbeatable mode, the AI plays a perfect game every time. The best outcome a human can achieve against a perfect AI is a draw — never a win.

🎲

Easy Mode

The AI occasionally makes a random move instead of the optimal one, giving you a fighting chance to win and experience the game in a different way.
