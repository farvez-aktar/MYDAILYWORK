Tips for Best Results

  • ✅ Use a well-lit, clear front-facing photo
  • ✅ Single face per enrollment image
  • ✅ Enroll 2–5 images per person for better accuracy
  • ❌ Avoid sunglasses, heavy shadows, or extreme angles
  • ❌ Avoid blurry or low-resolution images
How it works
Your face is encoded into a 512-dimensional ArcFace embedding vector. Recognition compares cosine distance between embeddings – no raw images are used for matching.
